Exitecs = Experts to deliver best-value Information Technology Services

EXITECS specializes in engineering and technology consulting. The company was founded in 2010 by consultants from major consulting firms.

The support provided to clients spans the entire project lifecycle, from consulting to TMA (Application Maintenance and Support), including implementation. The goal is to bring maximum value to clients, achieved through true innovation in the management of Information Systems Departments' processes.

• In terms of the efficiency of the client's internal processes: industrialization, procurement, contracting, urban planning, outsourcing, change management, as well as accounting.

• In terms of the value added to the business, by improving alignment between Business processes and application chains.

EXITECS commits to delivering results, whether through fixed-price missions or success fees.
